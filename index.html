VantaDraw at a Glance

System Purpose

VantaDraw is a high-performance digital drafting suite optimized for **architectural sketching**, **mandala creation**, and **technical illustration**. It eliminates the friction of traditional drawing by utilizing a unique hover-to-draw interaction model.

Target Audience

Engineered for architects, symmetry artists, and digital minimalists who require a distraction-free, "No-Glow" environment to focus on form, geometry, and technical precision.
